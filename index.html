About Us

History Of Venezia

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Hawassa, on Street 05, Venezia Italian Restaurantis more than just a destination—it's a story of love, culture, and culinary artistry. Established in 2002 E.C. by the charming duo, Italian Chef Mr. Andreya and Ethiopian Chef Ms. Wenarge, Venezia is the harmonious blend of two worlds. With years of experience in Italy's finest kitchens, this dynamic couple set out to create a haven where Italian elegance meets the warm, spicy soul of Ethiopian cuisine.
